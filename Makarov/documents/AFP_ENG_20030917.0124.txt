Car bomb blast in Denmark kills one: police
COPENHAGEN, Sept 17 (AFP)
A bomb explosion destroyed a car Wednesday, killing one person outside a hospital in Glostrup, Denmark, police said.
Officials did not say whether the vehicle had been booby-trapped, or whether the explosion was caused by the occupant, nor did they give any details about the victim.
The vehicle was parked outside the hospital chapel. No other injuries were reported.
